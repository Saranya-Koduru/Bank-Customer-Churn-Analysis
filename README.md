# Bank-Customer-Churn-Analysis
An interactive Microsoft Excel dashboard that analyzes customer churn drivers and provides actionable business insights to support customer retention strategies.

## 📌 Project Overview

ABC Bank has experienced a rise in customer churn, resulting in reduced revenue, increased customer acquisition costs, and lower long-term profitability.
Management requires a data-driven understanding of:
Which customers are leaving
Why they are leaving
How churn can be reduced through targeted retention strategies

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The primary objectives of this project are to:

- Identify customers with the highest churn risk
- Analyze the factors influencing customer attrition
- Develop an interactive dashboard for business decision-making
- Provide data-driven recommendations to improve customer retention

---

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

- Customers aged **38–77 years** exhibit approximately **3× higher churn** than younger customers.
- **Germany** records the highest churn rate among all regions.
- **Inactive customers** are nearly twice as likely to churn as active customers.
- Customers with **one banking product** show significantly higher churn.
- Account balance demonstrates a moderate relationship with churn.
- Credit score shows minimal influence on customer attrition.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Prioritize customer retention initiatives in Germany.
- Develop engagement programs targeting customers aged 38 years and above.
- Re-engage inactive customers through personalized campaigns.
- Promote cross-selling strategies for single-product customers.
- Improve source data quality and collect additional behavioural data for deeper churn analysis.
